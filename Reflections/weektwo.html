Week Two Reflection

What I think About My Home Page

I think my index page has an aesteically pleasing cool-toned color scheme that is perfect for summer because it makes me think of swimming. I added some personal effects to my logo, it is a butterfly with a hover-tilt effect to appear to "fly back home" to the index page. I have a butterfly tattoo on my arm that is half winged-half a bed of flowers, I got this tattoo six years ago in memory of my mother after she passed. I also added a photo slideshow with three different selfies. I think slideshows are a nice addition to the page so that it doesn't seem so plain.

I have included my GitHub link in the navigation bar, along with a dropdown menu for the lab projects and another drop down menu containing the weekly reflections.

How can it be improved?

I think I could have done a better job with the logo. I personally love it but people may not realize that it is a link back to my home page. I also could have made a better self description but I never know what to say about myself in these things.
